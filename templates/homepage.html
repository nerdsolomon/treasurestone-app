About School

Treasure Stone Schools is a dream come through born out of the need to address and redress the raising of independent and total learners, who would be problem solvers in all ramifications.

Treasure Stone School is currently a Nursery and Primary school complemented with well equipped, spacious and well ventilated classrooms for learning.

At Treasure Stone Schools, the staff work together as a team to ensure that every Treasure (Child) has a positive, happy and rewarding school experience towards becoming a global leader.

Vision

Learning to rise to be institution of excellence, achieving full potential and bringing out the treasures in every child.

Mission

Help learners discover, develop and nurture the rich treasures deposited in them through provision of qualitative learning ambience that will make them functionally valuable and relivant to their environment.
